## Public Product And Technical Positioning

智德萬 publicly positions itself around AI medical-imaging software and medical
AI workflow deployment.

Publicly listed capabilities include:

- automatic classification of multiple brain tumors in MRI;
- automatic segmentation / contouring of multiple brain tumors in MRI;
- brain arteriovenous malformation detection in MRI;
- hemodynamic-parameter estimation in MRI;
- coronary artery calcification detection in CT;
- physiological-signal analysis and identification;
- AI medical-device regulation and certification services.

The official website's DeepBT pages show two current product surfaces:

- `DeepBT Detector` - AI brain-tumor assisted detection system.
- `DeepBT Detector Plus` / `DeepBT Detector-Plus` - AI brain-tumor assisted
  analysis system for preoperative and postoperative multi-timepoint tumor
  detection / analysis.

The DeepBT Detector-Plus official description states that it combines an AI
brain-tumor analysis module with a DICOM-compliant viewer, uses a locked
algorithm design, accepts T1W+C or T1W+C plus T2W MR images, outputs lesion
contours, supports tumor volume-change calculation, and provides DICOM RTSS /
DICOM PR formats for downstream radiation-therapy and viewing workflows.

Important claim boundary:

- The official site repeatedly frames the product as physician assistance.
- The formal diagnosis remains with professional physicians and ordinary
  clinical workflow.
- This is directly relevant to our AI-triage wording: stay in assistive,
  staff-review, human-in-the-loop language; avoid autonomous diagnosis,
  autonomous triage, or final clinical-disposition claims.
